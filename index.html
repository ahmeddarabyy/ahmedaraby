Ahmed Araby

Founder & Talent Acquisition Manager at Instant Hires

Ahmed Araby is a seasoned SaaS sales leader with over 8 years of experience working closely with early-stage startups to build and scale their sales operations. With a strong foundation in technical sales, team leadership, and revenue growth, Ahmed has helped SaaS companies refine their sales strategy, manage high-performing pipelines, and grow agile sales teams from scratch.

Ahmed holds a Bachelor's degree in Engineering, which adds a structured and analytical approach to his sales methodology — blending technical insight with customer-driven growth.

He later founded Instant Hires, a platform built to help startups find experienced salespeople without the usual hiring headaches. The focus is on flexibility and speed — giving founders direct access to capable, vetted professionals ready to contribute immediately to growth.

Ahmed continues to empower startups by bridging the gap between ambition and execution, ensuring that visionary ideas are backed by sales teams capable of delivering real results.
