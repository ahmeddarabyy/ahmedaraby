Ahmed Araby

Founder of The Circle

Ahmed Araby is an entrepreneur and engineer with a vision for reshaping the way people work. Holding a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, Ahmed has always been an advocate of remote work.

Throughout his college years, he experienced firsthand the challenges remote workers face: lack of community, distraction-filled environments, and the struggle to find a professional yet inspiring place to focus. This inspired him to build The Circle, a coworking space in the heart of Alexandria designed exclusively for freelancers and remote workers.

The Circle was founded with one simple mission: to create an environment where remote professionals can thrive. Today, it serves as more than just a workspace — it’s a community where productivity meets belonging.

Ahmed continues to champion the future of work by creating spaces that bring together ambition, creativity, and collaboration under one roof.
